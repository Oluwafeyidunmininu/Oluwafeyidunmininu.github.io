Data Analysis On The Sustainable Jobs And Skills Against Inflation In Nigeria (2024)

This project analyzes inflation-resistant jobs and skills in Nigeria, leveraging Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

Excel SQL Power BI

Abstract

Introduction: This analysis investigates sustainable jobs and skills resistant to inflation in Nigeria (2024) amid rising inflation rates hitting 29.90% in January 2024. It aims to identify high-growth, well-paying jobs offering economic stability.

Data Collection: Data was sourced from authoritative Nigerian labor reports across three Excel sheets on jobs, skills, and inflation resistance.

Data Cleaning: Cleaned in Excel: duplicates removed, missing values handled, unnecessary columns dropped, headers renamed, and columns reorganized.

Table Joining: SQL merged the cleaned tables for unified analysis.

Data Visualization: Power BI presented job ratings, growth, salary, and inflation resistance.

Key Findings:

  • Inflation Resistance: Cyber-Security Specialist, Data Scientist, Medical Specialist, and Software Developer rank highest.
  • Growth Rate: Renewable Energy Engineer, Cyber-Security Specialist, and Data Scientist show rapid growth, with Renewable Energy Engineer offering a higher salary but lower inflation resistance.
  • Average Salary: Software Developer, Medical Specialist, and Data Scientist are the highest earners among the top jobs.
  • Job Ratings: Medical Specialist received the highest skill rating (5/5).
