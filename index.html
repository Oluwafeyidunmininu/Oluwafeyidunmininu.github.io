In this project, we analyze which jobs and skills can withstand inflationary pressures in Nigeria as of 2024. Using a combination of Excel for data cleaning, SQL for querying labor market datasets, and Power BI for interactive visualization dashboards, we identify sectors and skill sets resilient to inflation. The goal is to provide actionable insights to job seekers and policymakers to focus on sustainable employment opportunities.
